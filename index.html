About us

Welcome to Gifty's Touch Salon, where excellent customer service is paramount amongst our team of talented staff. The educated stylists and technicians offer exceptional hair services using state-of-the-art equipment for an experience that will exceed your expectations and all of this at competitive prices. With our customer’s loyalty we became more than a hair salon; we’re a warm and welcoming experience that will change the way you look at taking care of your hair. we pride ourselves on providing you with the best possible overall experience. Try a new look from our team of innovative hair designers, stylists and colorists. We look forward to serving you.
